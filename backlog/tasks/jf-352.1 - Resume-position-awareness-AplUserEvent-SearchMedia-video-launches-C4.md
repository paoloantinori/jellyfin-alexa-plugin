From JF-349 /code-review high (C4). AplUserEventHandler's Movie branch and SearchMediaIntentHandler's PlayItem video branch announce "Now playing X" unconditionally, with no UserData lookup — while PlayVideoIntentHandler announces "Resuming X from position Y" when resumeTicks > 0.

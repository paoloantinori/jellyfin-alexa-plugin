## Description

Harden the internet-facing anonymous surface required by the Alexa/LWA design: reflected XSS on the account-linking login page, unauthenticated media streaming, ffmpeg DoS levers, webhook replay protection, and token-storage/CSRF hardening. Findings from the 2026-07-12 security review (all verified against code).
